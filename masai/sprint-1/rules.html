:Rules & Regulation:

Here are the rules and regulations. All players are advised to read all the rules and regulations before participating. Summary: · For all events preliminary round , the game will be in either league or knock-out format. The format will be use as follows: League - play 1 game, each game 21 points, all points counted, 11 points change court, the most win top the group and advanced to the next round. · For all events second round and above (including quater-finals, semi-finals and finals): Knock-out - play best of 3 games, each game 21 points, all points counted.
